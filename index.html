Blue Origin

Suborbital flight

Blue Origin's suborbital rocket New Shepard on July 20, 2021, for the first time in the company's history, launched a capsule with people to space height, with the richest man in the world, Jeff Bezos, on board.

The New Shepard, 18 m high and 4 m wide, is a reusable spacecraft. The booster unit raises a sealed passenger vessel to an altitude of about 76 km. The impulse propels the capsule up into space.

After that, she descends to the ground under three parachutes. The booster is also returning home.
